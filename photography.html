Frequently Asked Questions

What is your turnaround time?

Typically 2-3 weeks for most sessions, but rush delivery is available upon request.

Do you travel for shoots?

Yes! Travel fees may apply depending on the distance from the city center.

How do we receive our photos?

You will receive a link to a secure online gallery where you can download high-res files.

What should I wear?

Wear something you feel comfortable and confident in. I recommend solid colors and avoiding large logos.

Can I have the RAW files?

I do not provide RAW files as the editing process is half of my creative work.

What happens if it rains?

We can reschedule or embrace the weather! Cloudy days actually provide beautiful, soft lighting.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, a 50% non-refundable retainer is required to secure your date.

Can we order prints through you?

Absolutely! The online gallery has an integrated store for high-quality professional prints.

How long have you been a photographer?

I've been shooting professionally for several years, constantly evolving my style.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, for larger packages like weddings or full commercial days, we can work out a schedule.
